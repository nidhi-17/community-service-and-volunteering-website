Volunteers are an integral part of many organizations and nonprofits. In fact, a volunteer team can be the difference between meeting your annual goals and not. Like any other part of an organization, they require tools and resources to work effectively and efficiently. At iVolunteer, we work with nonprofit organizations of all sizes to help manage and empower volunteers in less time through our robust volunteer management solution for programs and events. InitLive is committed to ensuring that every organization is equipped with the right tools to increase its impact and meet its goals.


Upcoming Events:

5K Walk/Run

Marathons around the world have a very unique and powerful energy, and this energy brings something different for different people. For some individuals, a Marathon is a fun event, to jog for 5-6 km on the streets with their friends, and soak in the feeling of a carnival-like joy. For some, a Marathon is an opportunity to prove their athletic prowess, push their personal limits and attempt to be among the best long-distance runners.

Bake Sale Fundraising Event

This is a classic food fundraising idea for a reason! Get ready for some sweet smells with this idea. Participants compete to bake the most delectable treat out there, and guests can donate for the chance to taste test and vote for their favorites. Movie Night

Hosting a film screening or movie marathon at a local park or auditorium and sell tickets and snacks for suggested donations. Another twist to this familiar fundraising event is to ask friends and family to each host their own mini-film screenings on behalf of your cause and invite their networks. They can then speak about your mission afterward and take note of those who want to learn more.
Gardening day

A gardening day out is a healthy option if the weather allows for it. Have everyone bring their gardening tools and set out to revitalise a local green space. Gardening connects people to the earth, and allows for moderate exercise in the sunshine and fresh air. It provides a sense of calm and accomplishment and affords the opportunity to create something of great beauty and/or bounty. any sort depending on your interest and location, all of which are fun and creative.
